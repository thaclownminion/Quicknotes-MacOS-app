# Quicknotes-MacOS-app
This app allows you to make notes in the menu bar. 

# Key features
- Take notes in your menu bar
- import .md, .txt files
- export your notes as a .txt file
- open your recent imported and made files
- clear your recents

# How to use
When you want to take notes, just press on the notes icon in your menu bar. Take your notes and if you want to save your notes just press the "save" button and select the folder where you want to put the files in

## importing
When you want to import your files you made in another app just press the "import" button on the top of your note taking interface and just select the file. 

### disclaimer
You will only be able to import .md and .txt files

## Quitting the app
Press on the "Recents" on the bottom left and press the "Quit" button on the top right next to the "Back" button.
